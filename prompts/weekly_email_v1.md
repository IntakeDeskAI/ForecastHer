## Email Structure

### Subject Line
- Write 3 subject line options (A/B/C test ready)
- Max 50 characters each
- Must be curiosity-driven, not clickbait

### Preview Text
- 1 line that complements the subject (shows in inbox preview)
- Max 90 characters

### Header
- Brief greeting (1-2 sentences)
- Tone: friendly, insider-y, like a smart friend catching you up

### This Week's Markets (main section)
- Feature 3-5 markets with:
  - Market question
  - Current illustrative odds (if mode=prelaunch, label as illustrative)
  - 1-sentence "why it matters"
  - Resolution date
- Use bullet format for scannability

### What We're Watching
- 2-3 emerging trends or news items relevant to women's health/femtech
- Brief context (1-2 sentences each)
- Link to source

### Community Spotlight (optional)
- Highlight if there's a notable community suggestion or poll result
- Skip if no data

### CTA
- Primary: Join the waitlist / Explore the preview
- Secondary: Follow on social

### Footer
- {{disclosure}}
- Unsubscribe link placeholder
- ForecastHer, Boise, Idaho

## Rules
- No medical advice
- No invented metrics
- All odds labeled as "illustrative" in prelaunch mode
- Citations for any factual claims
- Warm but professional tone
